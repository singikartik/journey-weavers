Mobile adaptation · Option 01

What changes on a phone.

  • 01
    Display type stays, scale drops
    Cormorant Garamond holds; the 128px hero becomes 56px. Same italic cadence, same em accents.
  • 02
    Grids collapse to stacks
    Two-column verticals & four-up stats become tabs + card stacks. Section rhythm is preserved, not flattened.
  • 03
    Single CTA per fold
    Primary action always within thumb reach. Full-width buttons, 52px hit area, 20-24px side padding.
  • 04
    Hero imagery below the fold
    Type-first. Image appears as a 4:5 portrait beneath the first CTA so the text is never fighting a photo.
  • 05
    Sticky, quiet nav
    Translucent paper background with blur. Logo + burger only — the whole menu lives behind the burger.
For the Figma handoff When we lock the direction, every page gets two artboards — Desktop (1440) and Mobile (375). Breakpoint rules, spacing tokens, and component states are documented alongside each frame so your developers can build without guesswork.
